Silas leaves the yard behind and walks into the Echo House proper, where memories are currency.

## Scene

The corridor was lined with glass doors, each one flickering with the afterimage of a life traded away. Silas paused at the threshold, feeling the weight of every memory he’d ever bartered.

He stepped inside, boots echoing on the polished floor. The house was alive with whispers—transactions, regrets, and the faint hum of the Psyntuum’s machinery.

Somewhere deeper, Paulo waited, and the storm outside was nothing compared to the one inside these walls.
